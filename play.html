How to play

The recommended way to play on wings is to use our official installer, available on our Discord.

It is also possible to connect using the standard Topaz bootloader by specifying --server game.wingsxi.com
Please note however using a different client version may cause some issues, including:
  • Wrong NPC names
  • Cutscenes hanging, which in some cases may require restarting the client
  • Certain values such as conquest point item purchase price, spell MP cost and others will be incorrect
Due to the above, bug reports will only be accepted when using the official client.

To create an account, use the signup link on the left menu. Your website credentials are also used to log-in to the game. It is not possible to register an account through the bootloader.

Enjoy your stay!
